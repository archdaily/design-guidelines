1. Centered tooltips

If you have a text and this text needs to be explaied, just hover the question mark. And here you get the explanation

The class 'afd-tips__txt--bottom' makes the tooltip appears below the question mark.

2. Right tooltips

But you also can make appear the tooltip at the right of the question mark replacing the class 'afd-tips__txt--bottom' by the class 'afd-tips__txt--right' And the tooltip appears by the right

Text Right tooltip

{% endhighlight %}

3. Left tooltips

Obviously you can also make appear the tooltip at the left of the question mark replacing the class 'afd-tips__txt--bottom' by the class 'afd-tips__txt--left' And the tooltip appears by the left
