1.1. Format

1.1.1. JPG

Images with complex gradients as a portrait or a landscape have a better compression on this format. To have a perseption of faster launch of the image use progresive compression and save the images with a quality between 80% and 90% trying to don't lose color richness.

  • Format: JPG
  • Compression: Progresive
  • Quality 80% - 90%
